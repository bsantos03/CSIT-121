Lasagna Recipe

Bryan Santos' most tasteful lasagna recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 3 cloves of minced garlic
  • 24 ounces of tomato sauce
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic in a large skillet over medium heat until the beef is browned.
  3. Add the tomato sauce to the skillet with the beef and let it simmer for roughly around 10 to 15 minutes.
  4. Cook the lasagna noodles
  5. Drain and rinse with cold water
  6. Now, in a different bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, egg, 1 cup of mozzarella cheese, parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper.
  7. Layer a thin platform of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  8. Place lasagna noodles on top of the sauce. Just a layer enough to cover the meat sauce.
  9. Spread ricotta cheese mixture on top of the noodles.
  10. Repeat layering the meat sauce, lasagna, and cheese. Stacking each other on top of each other.
  11. Sprinkle a cup of mozzarella cheese on top of the final layer.
  12. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  13. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25-30 minutes, until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

This is my favorite recipe because this recipe is very easy to make and it is very tasty.
